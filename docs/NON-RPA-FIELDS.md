# Non-RPA Fields Kept on the Deep Link Form

**Version:** 1.0.0  |  **Date:** 2026-02-18

These fields are not part of the C.A.R. RPA but are intentionally included on the VoicePilot deep link form for workflow completeness.

| # | Field | Section | Reason Kept |
|---|-------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Seller Name | Sellers & Agents | Agent workflow convenience (RPA fills seller info at acceptance, not at offer) |
| 2 | Seller Email | Sellers & Agents | Agent workflow convenience |
| 3 | Seller Phone | Sellers & Agents | Agent workflow convenience |
| 4 | Escalation Increment (above competing offer) | Escalation Clause | Additional terms functionality for competitive offers |
| 5 | Maximum Price Cap | Escalation Clause | Additional terms functionality for competitive offers |
| 6 | Escalation Clause Text | Escalation Clause | Additional terms functionality for competitive offers |
| 7 | Compensation Type (% of Purchase Price, Flat Fee, Seller Paid, Hourly, Other) | BRBC | Expanded from separate BRBC form for workflow completeness |
| 8 | Retainer Amount | BRBC | Expanded from separate BRBC form |
| 9 | Retainer Credited at Close | BRBC | Expanded from separate BRBC form |
| 10 | Exclusive Representation | BRBC | Expanded from separate BRBC form |
| 11 | BRBC Expiration Date | BRBC | Expanded from separate BRBC form |
| 12 | BRBC Executed Date | BRBC | Expanded from separate BRBC form |
| 13 | Compensation Notes | BRBC | Expanded from separate BRBC form |
| 14 | Termite Inspection Paid By | Disclosures & Inspections | Commonly negotiated, useful despite not being in RPA schema |
| 15 | Initial Deposit (%) | Price & Deposits | Calculated convenience field |
| 16 | Seller Credit (%) | Price & Deposits | Calculated convenience field |
| 17 | Down Payment (%) | Financing | Calculated convenience field |

**Total:** 17 non-RPA fields retained
